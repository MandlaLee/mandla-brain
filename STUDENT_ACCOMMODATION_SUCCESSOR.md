# Student Accommodation Successor

The next student-accommodation platform does not have a final name yet. Do not invent one, and do not automatically reuse SamLee Estates or Uni Residentia.

## Current direction

Build a newly branded student-accommodation business and information platform that replaces the existing SamLee website generations while keeping their strongest idea: a large, genuinely useful multi-page SEO architecture.

The many pages are deliberate. Preserve useful clusters for:

- UFS and CUT students
- Bloemfontein locations
- NSFAS questions
- affordability and room types
- first-year, postgraduate and parent concerns
- application guidance
- moving, budgeting, safety and university-life resources

## Historical website references

- `MandlaLee/SamLee` — preserve its broad SEO structure and university/resource hub thinking.
- `MandlaLee/samlee-estates-1.2` — preserve useful repair work, accessibility, metadata and application-integration patterns.
- `MandlaLee/samlee2` — preserve its combined SEO breadth and conversion-focused pages.

All three are predecessors, not the final current website.

## Competitive research

CampusKey is being studied through a private SiteSucker mirror of its publicly delivered website. Extract useful information architecture, customer journeys and presentation principles, then build an original improved implementation for the future new brand.

## Uni Residentia

Uni Residentia was registered through CIPC in 2025 but was never used operationally. Mandla no longer has the original account access details, and its present company status is unknown.

Before using Uni Residentia for anything, verify its CIPC status and whether access can be recovered. It is not automatically the replacement brand.
